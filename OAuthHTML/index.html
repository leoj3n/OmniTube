Commands

There are many features in OmniTube. Get the general overview below!

Script Filters

YouProfile is a command that allows you to view your YouTube profile's information.

YouFeed displays your subscription's newest uploads.

YouVideo searches YouTube for any video you like.

YouChannel searches YouTube for a specific channel.

YouPlaylist searches YouTube for any playlist you specify.

YouHistory displays your recent video views.

YouPopular displays the most popular videos of today.

Keyboard Shortcuts

CTRL will usually allow you to add or remove a video from your Watch Later playlist.

ALT will usually allow you to add or remove a video from your Favorites.

CMD will allow you to download the video if you my workflow (Luxinate) installed.

FN will copy the URL of the current selected video to your clipboard.
